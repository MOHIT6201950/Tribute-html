About A. P. J Abdul Kalam

A. P. J Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

Contributions

  • Played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Contributed to the development of India's space and missile programs.
  • Authored several books, including "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds".
